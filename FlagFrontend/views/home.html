Welcome to Flag Rush!

Flag Rush is a strategy board game, this game was inspired by Game of the Generals. This game puts your intellect to the test. Play now and become the ultimate Flag Master!

Basic Rules

  • To win a player flag must make it to the last row at the opposing side of the board.
  • You lose if your flag gets eliminated by an enemy piece.
  • You eliminate the opposing player's piece by rank. If your piece is higher ranked then you eliminate the enemy's piece.
  • To attack you need to place your piece on a tile that has an opposing piece.
  • If your piece has the same rank as the enemy piece you both lose the piece.

Computer

  • Only the computer will know the rank of both players pieces.
  • The role of the computer is to determine who wins the battle between two pieces. So that the rank is never revealed to the player.

Moves

  • Each piece can move 1 tile to the right, left ,forward, or backwards. No diagonal moves allowed or moves with 2 spaces are not allowed.
  • Enemy Pieces will not move , the players flag must navigate its way through the enemy lines.

Positioning

  • A player will get 12 pieces and a FLAG to use for their army.
  • At the begininning of the game you get to position all your pieces in the first 3 rows on your side.
  • Since rank of the enemy piece is never revealed you can conclude that if you lose the piece, that piece rank is higher than the piece you lost. (Vice Versa)

Pieces and Rank

  • Flag (no star): 1 piece
  • King (6 star): 2 piece
  • Queen (5 star): 2 piece
  • Duke (4 star): 2 piece
  • Counts (3 star): 2 piece
  • VisCount (2 star): 2 piece
  • Knight (1 star): 2 piece
